## Storage and Preservation

Proper storage maintains the potency and safety of **White Maeng Da Kratom** over extended periods, protecting your investment and ensuring consistent effects.

### Optimal Storage Conditions

**Temperature Control**: Store kratom in cool, stable temperatures between 60-70°F. Avoid areas with temperature fluctuations like garages or attics.

**Humidity Management**: Maintain low humidity levels (below 50%) to prevent mold growth and alkaloid degradation. Use silica gel packets in storage containers if necessary.

**Light Protection**: Keep kratom away from direct sunlight and UV exposure, which can break down active compounds over time.

**Air Exposure**: Minimize oxygen contact by using airtight containers and avoiding frequent opening of storage vessels.

### Container Selection

Different storage options offer varying levels of protection:

**Vacuum-Sealed Bags**: Excellent for long-term storage, removing air and preventing oxidation.

**Original Packaging**: If properly sealed, vendor packaging often provides adequate short-term storage.
